Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about our workshops, courses, and events. If you need more help, feel free to contact us!

What is Canies Academy?

Canies Academy is an educational initiative offering skill-based workshops and training programs in technology, finance, and design to prepare students for real-world careers.

Who can attend the workshops?

Our workshops are designed for students from Grade 8 to college-level learners who want to enhance their technical and creative skills.

How do I register for a workshop?

You can register for our workshops through our official website. Registration links and details are provided on the event pages.

What is the duration of each course?

Our courses vary in duration. Workshops last 4-5 hours, courses extend up to 2-3 months.

Do you offer certifications?

Yes! Participants who complete our courses and workshops receive an official certification from Canies Academy.

How can I contact Canies Academy for more details?

You can reach out to us via email at caniesacademy@gmail.com or follow our social media for updates.
