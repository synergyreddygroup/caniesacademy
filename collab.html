Canies Academy: Empowering Future Tech Leaders

About Us

Canies Academy is a forward-thinking ed-tech institution specializing in hands-on, practical training programs in cutting-edge fields such as Artificial Intelligence, Robotics, Cybersecurity, and more. We are driven by the mission to equip students with the industry-relevant skills and innovation mindset essential for the future of technology. Our courses are led by seasoned industry experts and our CEO, Mr. Shrikanteshwar Reddy, whose entrepreneurial journey and technical expertise inspire and guide every training session.

Our Offerings

  • Industry-Ready Curriculum: A comprehensive course originally valued at ₹25,000, delivered at a nominal fee of ₹2,500.
  • Hands-On Practical Training: Interactive labs and real-world projects that allow students to apply technology concepts directly.
  • Direct Mentorship: Guidance from industry professionals including direct sessions with our CEO and other experts.
  • Exclusive Events: Participation in major events like Telangana’s biggest AI & Robotics Expo, giving students exposure to the latest trends and networking opportunities.
  • Personalized Project Kits: Each participant receives a hands-on kit designed to support practical project development.
  • Certification and Career Guidance: Official certification upon completion and continuous support to guide students toward promising tech careers.

What We Need from You

In return for delivering our advanced training program at your institution, we seek:

Publicity & Marketing Support:

  • Featuring the Canies Academy brand (logo and collateral) in your summer camp promotions, admission marketing materials, and school communication channels.

Exclusive Partnership Commitment:

  • A collaborative agreement that designates your school as the exclusive location for our courses in your designated zone, ensuring focused, impactful learning in your community.

Facility Support:

  • Provision of a suitable venue and necessary infrastructure (like a classroom or lab space) during the designated training sessions that align with your academic calendar.

What You Gain

By collaborating with Canies Academy, your institution will:

Enhance Student Learning:

  • Provide access to high-quality, industry-aligned courses that complement the standard curriculum and prepare students for future careers.

4. Draft MoU – Key Collaboration Terms

Here's a Draft Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) between Canies Academy and a school. This version is flexible and professional, perfect for initial collaboration agreements.

MEMORANDUM OF UNDERSTANDING (MoU)

This MoU is made on [Date]

Between:

Canies Academy, represented by Mr. Shrikanteshwar Reddy (CEO), hereinafter referred to as the "Training Partner",

And

[School Name], represented by [Principal's Name] (Principal), hereinafter referred to as the "Institution".

1. Purpose of the MoU:

This MoU outlines a strategic collaboration between Canies Academy and [School Name] to enhance student learning by offering practical training in emerging technologies like Artificial Intelligence, Robotics, Cybersecurity, Web Development, and more during summer and academic enrichment programs.

2. Responsibilities of Canies Academy (Training Partner):

  • Deliver hands-on training through expert-led workshops and courses.
  • Provide industry-level curriculum, project kits, and certification for participants.
  • Offer a course worth ₹25,000 at a nominal cost of ₹2,500 per student.
  • Provide direct mentorship by industry professionals, including CEO Mr. Shrikanteshwar Reddy.
  • Invite selected students to participate in Telangana's Largest AI & Robotics Expo.
  • Ensure high-quality delivery, student engagement, and safety during sessions.

3. Responsibilities of [School Name] (Institution):

  • Provide venue, infrastructure (classrooms/projectors), and basic coordination support.
  • Assist in promoting the program during summer camps and new admissions.
  • Allow the inclusion of Canies Academy's branding on flyers, banners, and promotional materials.
  • Share the collaboration details in school circulars, websites, and social media.
  • Ensure exclusive collaboration within their school zone (Canies Academy will not partner with nearby competitors).

4. Branding & Publicity Agreement:

  • Canies Academy's logo will be displayed in all relevant promotional material for the summer camps or academic year.
  • Joint branding will be featured in hoardings, flyers, and digital campaigns run by the Institution.
  • Any content shared on social media about the program will tag and credit both parties.

5. Term & Termination:

This MoU shall be effective from [Start Date] and shall remain valid for a period of 1 year, unless renewed or terminated by either party with a 15-day prior written notice.

6. Confidentiality:

Both parties agree to maintain the confidentiality of proprietary information and not disclose such information without prior consent.

7. Legal Understanding:

This MoU is not legally binding but reflects the intention of both parties to work together in good faith.

Any dispute will be resolved through mutual discussion.

A. Digital Marketing

Website Integration:
  • Add a dedicated section on [School Name]’s website that explains the partnership and showcases the benefits of the joint programs.

Local Media & Press Releases:
  • Draft a joint press release that highlights the unique collaboration and its benefits to community education.
  • Engage local newspapers, online education portals, and radio stations to cover the success stories and upcoming events.

C. Branding & Co-Branding

  • Ensure all marketing materials include the logos, color schemes, and branding elements of both Canies Academy and the school.
  • Agree on placement, size, and usage guidelines for logos in all collateral, banners, and digital materials.
Joint Sponsorships & Partnerships:
