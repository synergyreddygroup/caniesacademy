Canies Academy – Collaboration Documents

1. Company Profile / Brochure

Canies Academy is a futuristic skill development initiative aimed at empowering school students (Classes 8–12) with practical, job-ready knowledge in the fields of AI, Robotics, Programming, and emerging technologies. With expert-led mentorship, cutting-edge projects, and interactive kits, our programs ensure deep hands-on learning.

  • Vision: Democratize future tech education for every student.
  • Mission: Equip students with essential tech skills, industry exposure, and confidence to innovate.
  • Unique Offerings: Industry mentorship, AI-powered kits, real-time simulations, career counselling, and AI & Robotics Expo participation.

2. Course Curriculum Outline (3 Months)

Target Audience: Class 8–12 students
Duration: 3 Months (12 Weeks)
Class Frequency: 1 class per week (3 hours each)

Week Topic
1 Introduction to AI and ML – Concepts, Applications, Real-world Impact
2 Basics of Machine Learning – Supervised & Unsupervised Learning
3 Prompt Engineering – Crafting Inputs for Generative AI (ChatGPT, etc.)
4 Prompt Engineering – Advanced Techniques, Real-world Prompts
5 Introduction to C++ – Syntax, Variables, Operators
6 C++ for Arduino – Loops, Conditionals, Functions
7 Arduino Hardware Overview – IDE Setup, Basic LED Projects
8 Arduino Sensors and Actuators – Practical Integration
9 IoT Fundamentals – Cloud, Connectivity, Applications
10 Networking Basics – IP, WiFi Modules, Network Simulations
11 Robotics Basics – Types of Robots, Sensors, Logic
12 Robotics Simulations – Tinkercad, Virtual Robot Building & Control

4. Draft MoU – Key Collaboration Terms

Here's a Draft Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) between Canies Academy and a school. This version is flexible and professional, perfect for initial collaboration agreements.

MEMORANDUM OF UNDERSTANDING (MoU)

This MoU is made on [Date]

Between:

Canies Academy, represented by Mr. Shrikanteshwar Reddy (CEO), hereinafter referred to as the "Training Partner",

And

[School Name], represented by [Principal’s Name] (Principal), hereinafter referred to as the "Institution".

1. Purpose of the MoU:

This MoU outlines a strategic collaboration between Canies Academy and [School Name] to enhance student learning by offering practical training in emerging technologies like Artificial Intelligence, Robotics, Cybersecurity, Web Development, and more during summer and academic enrichment programs.

2. Responsibilities of Canies Academy (Training Partner):

  • Deliver hands-on training through expert-led workshops and courses.
  • Provide industry-level curriculum, project kits, and certification for participants.
  • Offer a course worth ₹25,000 at a nominal cost of ₹2,500 per student.
  • Provide direct mentorship by industry professionals, including CEO Mr. Shrikanteshwar Reddy.
  • Invite selected students to participate in Telangana's Largest AI & Robotics Expo.
  • Ensure high-quality delivery, student engagement, and safety during sessions.

3. Responsibilities of [School Name] (Institution):

  • Provide venue, infrastructure (classrooms/projectors), and basic coordination support.
  • Assist in promoting the program during summer camps and new admissions.
  • Allow the inclusion of Canies Academy’s branding on flyers, banners, and promotional materials.
  • Share the collaboration details in school circulars, websites, and social media.
  • Ensure exclusive collaboration within their school zone (Canies Academy will not partner with nearby competitors).

4. Branding & Publicity Agreement:

  • Canies Academy’s logo will be displayed in all relevant promotional material for the summer camps or academic year.
  • Joint branding will be featured in hoardings, flyers, and digital campaigns run by the Institution.
  • Any content shared on social media about the program will tag and credit both parties.

5. Term & Termination:

This MoU shall be effective from [Start Date] and shall remain valid for a period of 1 year, unless renewed or terminated by either party with a 15-day prior written notice.

6. Confidentiality:

Both parties agree to maintain the confidentiality of proprietary information and not disclose such information without prior consent.

7. Legal Understanding:

This MoU is not legally binding but reflects the intention of both parties to work together in good faith.

Any dispute will be resolved through mutual discussion.
